About Us


Want to help abandoned or injured animals? Relax Harbour has got your back. We provide vet services as well as shelter for domestic animals. This is an initiative to save these innocent lives. With the help of volunteers we try to solve this problem on mass level. Want to be a part of this initiative. Join us and help us make a difference.



Our Misssion


  • To rescue injured animals and provide medical and other necessary facilities.
  • To provide food and shelter to abandoned animals.
  • To help people adopt these cute little creatures.
