Tautology

Noun

Meaning : the saying of the same thing twice over in different words, generally considered to be a fault of style.

Synonym's Antonym's
repetition conciseness
redundancy directness
superfluity straightforwardness
duplication terseness

Examples :

  • Commenters like to describe this as a beautiful tautology..
  • Safdie filmmaking fulfills a boys-will-be-boys tautology..
