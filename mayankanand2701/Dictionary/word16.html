Pithy

Adjective

Meaning : expressed in a clear, direct way

Synonym's Antonym's
aphoristic circuitous
brief diffuse
compact prolix
crisp rambling
concise verbose

Examples :

  • I read my poems in L.A., chatted up the literary set, waxed pithy and beleaguered at the book signings and wine and cheese receptions.
  • The critic gave the film a pithy review.
