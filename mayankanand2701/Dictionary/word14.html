Unctuous

Adjective

Meaning : excessively flattering or ingratiating; oily

Synonym's Antonym's
sycophantic artless
ingratiating candid
obsequious undesigning
fawning honest
self-abasing heartfelt

Examples :

  • An unctuous effort to appear religious to the voters.
  • An unctuous appraisal of the musical talent shown by the boss's daughter.
