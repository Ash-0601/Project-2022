Deciduous

Adjective

Meaning : of a type that loses its leaves every autumn

Synonym : brief,ephemeral,evanescent,flash,fleeting,impermanent,etc..

Antonym : ceaseless,dateless,unending,evergreen,persistent,abiding,constant,etc..

Synonym's Antonym's
brief ceaseless
ephemeral dateless
flash evergreen
fleeting constant

Examples :

  • The bare branches of a deciduous tree in winter.
  • He chose not to fret about the deciduous discomforts of his existence.
