Bowdlerize

Verb

Meaning : remove material that is considered improper or offensive from (a text or account), especially with the result that the text becomes weaker or less effective.

Synonym's Antonym's
cut expand
edit thicken
redact inflate

Examples :

  • The version of the play that I saw had been horribly bowdlerized.
  • The subject was how your parents, to bowdlerise Philip Larkin, mess you up.
