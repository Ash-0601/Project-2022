Extrinsic

Adjective

Meaning : not part of the essential nature of someone or something; coming or operating from outside.

Synonym's Antonym's
external inherent
exterior innate
foreign intrinsic

Examples :

  • You have to consider anyextrinsic factors in the success of the business.
  • The fact that the ring belonged to your grandmother is extrinsic to its value to a jeweler.
