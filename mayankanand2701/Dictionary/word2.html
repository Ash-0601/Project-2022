Esoteric

Adjective

Meaning : intended for or likely to be understood or enjoyed by only few people with a special knowledge or interest

Synonym's Antonym's
abstruse shallow
deep superficial

Examples :

  • A kahuna is a master of Hawaiian esoteric practices. Recently, Mariko Gordon and Hugh Cosman engaged a kahuna to bless their house.
  • There was a new mall, an excellent bookstore with esoteric literary and policy journals, some restaurants with cosmopolitan menus, and engaging real estate advertisements.
