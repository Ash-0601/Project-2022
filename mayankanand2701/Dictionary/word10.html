Pragmatic

Adjective

Meaning : dealing with problems in a practical way rather than by following ideas or principles

Synonym's Antonym's
down-to-earth blue-sky
earthy idealistic
hardheaded impractical
realistic utopian

Examples :

  • His pragmatic view of public education comes from years of working in city schools.
  • Many honor the seasonality and history of the dish, while others are more pragmatic.
